Marginal Cost of Goods

Python Streamlit

When 2 axies are bred together, cryptocurrency is paid in exchange for an egg. Two Cryptocurrencies are always used: SLP (higher breed count = more expensive) and AXS (flat amount). Cryptocurrencies are volatile but higly correlated, so ETH or BTC is useful as base currency for accounting purposes. My streamlit app gets USD price data from market_basic_stats (see databases), and visualises breed costs 1-7 in terms of ETH.
